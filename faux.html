Hindu Marriage Act, 1955

Hindu Marriage Act, 1955

This act governs the solemnization and registration of Hindu marriages. It also deals with issues like divorce, maintenance, and custody of children among Hindus. It applies to Hindus, Buddhists, Jains, and Sikh.

Dissolution of Muslim Marriages Act, 1939

Dissolution of Muslim Marriages Act, 1939

This act provides grounds and procedures for Muslim women to seek the dissolution of their marriages. It addresses issues such as divorce and maintenance under Islamic law.

Special Marriage Act, 1954

Special Marriage Act, 1954

The Special Marriage Act allows individuals from different religions or those wishing to marry outside their faith to do so. It provides for marriage registration and sets out the rules for interfaith marriages.

Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005

Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005

This act is not strictly a family law but is related to family matters. It aims to protect women from domestic violence and provides for restraining orders, maintenance, and residence orders.

Guardians and Wards Act, 1890

Guardians and Wards Act, 1890

This act deals with the appointment and powers of guardians for the care of minor children. It also outlines the responsibilities and authority of such guardians.
